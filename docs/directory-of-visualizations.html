9 Directory of visualizations

9.1 Individual distributions

Histograms and density plots (Chapter 11) provide the most intuitive visualizations of a distribution, but both require arbitrary parameter choices and can be misleading. Cumulative densities and q-q plots (Chapter 12) always represent the data faithfully but can be more difficult to interpret.
